Summary:

Tim Drake was Batman's third Robin. Where Jason was violent And reckless, Tim was cautious and smart. He is known as the most technologically capable Robin. A techno-wizard, if you will. He is also extremely smart.

Tim was introduced after Jason Todd's death. In his backstory, he managed to figure out the secret identities of both Batman and Nightwing. He also noticed that Batman was spiraling after Jason's death, and needed Robin. At the end of this arc, Tim was chosen to be the new Robin.

Compared to the other Robins, Tim lacked a traumatic background. And he even managed to have the most stable romantic relationship in the comics. Tim is dating Steph Brown (who was a Robin herself until the launch of the New 52 universe) and Connor Kent's punching through reality to save him was used as the basis for Jason's eventual revival
