Summary:

Dick Grayson was Batman's first Robin. If you're only a fan of the movies, I can understand where the confusion might come in. There's more than one robin? Yes, yes there are. But Dick is the most famous of the Robins, and if Robin makes an appearance in other media, it's a high chance that it's Dick.

In the 80s, Dick decided to strike out on his own. And borrowing from a Kryptonian legend, became the hero Nightwing.

Dick is the most popular of Batman's former sidekicks, among the family holds a place of honor. He's more upbeat and optimistic, compared especially to Jason. He also personally selected Tim to be the third Robin. And he is the only Robin to formally don Batman's cowl.
